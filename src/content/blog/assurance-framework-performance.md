Strong safety assurance frameworks are measurable. Without practical indicators, assurance quickly becomes a procedural exercise rather than an operational control system.

## Start with control confidence

Track whether key controls are both present and effective in real operating conditions. This should include evidence quality, not just checklist completion.

## Measure action closure quality

Closure speed matters, but closure quality matters more. Verify that actions remove root causes and are sustained over time.

## Track leadership assurance cadence

Safety assurance is strongest when governance forums consistently review meaningful leading and lagging indicators and make timely risk decisions.
